What qualifications do I need to go scuba diving with OkayDive?

You'll need to complete a basic scuba diving certification course, such as the PADI Open Water Diver certification, before participating in our dives. We offer training courses for beginners and advanced divers alike.

What should I bring for a dive trip with OkayDive?

We recommend bringing swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, and any personal items you may need. We provide all necessary scuba diving equipment, including wetsuits, masks, fins, and tanks.

What safety measures does OkayDive have in place during dives?

Safety is our top priority at OkayDive. Our experienced instructors conduct thorough safety briefings before each dive, ensure proper equipment setup and maintenance, and adhere to established diving protocols at all times.

Can I rent scuba diving equipment from OkayDive?

Yes, we offer rental equipment for divers who prefer not to bring their own gear. Our rental equipment is regularly serviced and maintained to ensure safety and reliability.

Are there age restrictions for scuba diving with OkayDive?

While there is no maximum age limit for scuba diving, participants must meet the minimum age requirements set by the certification agencies. Typically, participants must be at least 10 to 12 years old to start scuba diving training.

How long are the dive trips with OkayDive?

Dive trip durations vary depending on the location and type of dive. Typically, our dive trips last between 2 to 4 hours, including setup, briefing, diving, and debriefing.

What if I have medical concerns or physical limitations?

It's important to inform us of any medical conditions or physical limitations before participating in a dive. We'll work with you to assess your suitability for diving and provide appropriate accommodations if necessary.

Do you offer private or customized dive experiences?

Yes, we offer private and customized dive experiences for individuals, groups, and special occasions. Contact us to discuss your preferences and requirements, and we'll tailor a dive trip to suit your needs.
